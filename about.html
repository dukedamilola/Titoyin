Who we are

About Titoyin

Nigeria's trusted platform for news, media, and lifestyle — built for Nigerians, by Nigerians.

Our Mission

Titoyin was created with a single purpose: to give Nigerians a reliable, well-presented, and easily accessible source of news, analysis, and lifestyle content that reflects the reality of life in Nigeria while keeping readers connected to a broader world.

In a media environment full of noise, sensationalism, and unreliable sources, we are committed to presenting stories clearly, fairly, and responsibly — with proper sourcing, appropriate attribution, and editorial standards that protect our readers' trust.

We cover national events, politics, entertainment, fashion and style, trending stories, gossip, and selected international developments. Our goal is to be the first place Nigerians check when they want to know what is happening — and to trust what they find there.

Accuracy

We verify before we publish. Every story is reviewed for factual accuracy, and we correct errors promptly when they occur.

Fairness

We present multiple perspectives on contested issues and we do not allow personal opinion to distort news coverage.

Clarity

We write for real people, not for industry insiders. Our stories are clear, direct, and easy to understand.

Transparency

We are clear about what is news, what is analysis, what is opinion, and what is sponsored content. No hidden agendas.

Responsibility

We take seriously the power that comes with reaching a large audience and we use it carefully.

Privacy

We protect the privacy of our readers, our contributors, and the individuals we cover — to the fullest extent consistent with public interest journalism.

What We Cover

🇳🇬 National

Breaking news and analysis of events across all 36 states and the FCT — politics, security, economy, and society.

👗 Fashion & Style

Nigerian and international fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and trends — for the modern Nigerian reader.

🔥 Trending

The stories, moments, and conversations that Nigeria cannot stop talking about — captured in real time.

🌍 World

International events, African affairs, and global developments relevant to Nigerian readers and the diaspora.

Editorial Standards

Titoyin operates under a set of editorial guidelines that govern every piece of content we publish. These guidelines cover sourcing, attribution, conflict of interest, privacy, the handling of unverified information, and the labelling of different content types.

All news content is reviewed before publication. Sponsored content is clearly labelled and held to the same factual accuracy standards as editorial content. Opinion pieces are clearly identified as opinion and do not represent the position of the Titoyin editorial team.

We do not accept payment in exchange for editorial coverage. Advertising and editorial operations are kept strictly separate. Advertisers have no influence over editorial decisions.

If you believe we have made an error, please use our contact form to submit a correction request. We investigate all such requests and publish corrections prominently when they are warranted.
