Advertising Disclosure

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Titoyin is an independent publishing platform. In order to support our operations and continue providing free access to quality content, we display advertising and accept sponsored content on the Site. This disclosure explains how advertising and commercial content are handled.

Display Advertising

The Site displays banner and display advertisements in designated areas. These placements are sold directly to advertisers or served through advertising networks. Display advertisements are clearly positioned in designated ad zones and do not appear within editorial body text without labelling.

Sponsored Content

We may publish sponsored articles, promoted features, or branded content that have been paid for by an advertiser or commercial partner. All such content is clearly and prominently labelled as "Sponsored", "Promoted", "Advertising Feature", or similar designation at the top of the content. Sponsored content does not represent the editorial opinion of Titoyin.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial team operates independently of our advertising and commercial operations. Advertisers do not have influence over editorial coverage, story selection, or the factual content of news articles. The fact that an organisation advertises on our Site does not affect how or whether we cover that organisation editorially.

Affiliate Links

Some content on the Site may contain affiliate links through which we earn a commission if you make a purchase following that link. Where affiliate links are used, this will be disclosed in the relevant content.

Native Advertising

Any content that is presented in a format similar to editorial content but is paid for by an advertiser will be explicitly labelled to distinguish it from independent editorial content. We follow responsible native advertising standards to ensure readers are not misled.

Ad Choices

Advertising networks we use may employ cookies or similar technologies to serve interest-based advertisements. You may opt out of personalised advertising through browser settings or through the industry opt-out tools available in your region.

Contact

For advertising enquiries, please visit our advertising section. For questions about this disclosure, use our contact form.
