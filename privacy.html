Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025  ·  Effective date: 1 January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Titoyin ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit and use our website at titoyin.com ("the Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, referring pages, browser type, device type, and general geographic location (country/city level). This is collected automatically through analytics tools.
  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, and message content submitted through our contact form. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address provided when subscribing to our newsletter. Used only to send you the content you requested.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve site functionality and support analytics. See Section 5 for details.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as national identification numbers, financial information, or health information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information we collect is used to:

  • Operate and improve the website
  • Respond to contact form messages
  • Send newsletters to subscribers who have requested them
  • Analyse traffic patterns and improve content performance
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Meet our legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services including analytics providers and advertising networks. These services may collect information subject to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately. We use these services to understand how our content is performing and to serve relevant advertisements.

4. Advertising

We display advertisements on the Site. Advertisers and advertising networks may use cookies or other tracking technologies to serve advertisements relevant to your interests. You may opt out of interest-based advertising through your browser settings or through industry opt-out mechanisms available in your region.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to operate the website, remember your preferences, and support analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on the Site.

6. Data Retention

Contact form data is retained for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter. Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form as permitted by the analytics provider's policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights in relation to your personal information including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of information we hold about you. To exercise any such rights, please contact us through our contact form.

8. Children

Our Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please use our contact form and select "General Enquiry" or "Report Content" as appropriate.
